Yes, but do it carefully.

You should not just stuff words like “Dubizzle, DubiCars, cars for sale, cars Dubai, cars Sharjah” all over the page. That can look spammy and hurt you. Google’s own spam policy warns against manipulative tactics like keyword stuffing, and Google says content should be helpful for people, not mainly made to manipulate ranking.

The smart way is to create comparison / alternative pages.

For example:

/dubizzle-alternative-uae
/dubizzle-alternative-dubai
/dubicars-alternative-uae
/cars-for-sale-dubai
/cars-for-sale-sharjah
/cars-for-sale-uae
/sell-car-online-dubai
/sell-car-online-uae

For competitor keywords, use titles like:

Dubizzle Alternative for Cars and Spare Parts in the UAE | Saman Marketplace
DubiCars Alternative for UAE Car Listings | Saman Marketplace

But the page content must be fair and useful. Don’t say “better than Dubizzle” or make claims you can’t prove. Say:

Looking for another place to list your car or spare parts in the UAE? Saman Marketplace is a UAE automotive marketplace where users can post cars, spare parts, wheels, accessories, and automotive items.

For “cars for sale” searches, create clean location pages:

Cars for Sale in Dubai | Saman Marketplace
Cars for Sale in Sharjah | Saman Marketplace
Cars for Sale in Abu Dhabi | Saman Marketplace
Used Cars for Sale in UAE | Saman Marketplace

These are safer and more powerful than competitor pages because they match real buyer searches.
